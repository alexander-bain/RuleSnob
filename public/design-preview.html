Equipment Rules
A player's club conforms to the Rules. However, the player decides to modify the club. What is the ruling?
A
The player may modify the club before teeing off
B
The player must report the modification to the committee
C
The modified club no longer conforms; the player is disqualified
D
The player has already committed a violation
✓
Correct!
+8 points • Rules IQ: 64
Your Answer
C — The modified club no longer conforms; the player is disqualified
Correct Answer
C — The modified club no longer conforms; the player is disqualified
Rule Explanation
According to Rule 4.1a, a club must conform to the equipment rules. If a club is modified and no longer conforms, the player is in breach and faces disqualification under Rule 4.1b(1).
